About the Team

High Point University Baseball is coming off a historic, record-setting 2025 campaign. The Panthers finished 39–19 overall and 18–6 in Big South play, securing the top conference position and earning national recognition for one of the most explosive offenses in college baseball. Ranking among the top teams nationally in plate appearances per game, HPU produced multiple NCAA statistical leaders and one of the strongest offensive seasons in program history.

Junior infielder Landen Johnson led the charge with a .355 average, 75 hits, 18 doubles, 14 home runs, and 78 RBIs — finishing 3rd in the NCAA. Senior outfielder Brayden Simpson collected 24 doubles, while Konni Durschlag posted a staggering .434 AVG and 23 doubles to finish among the nation’s best hitters. Miggy Echazarreta added 17 doubles, rounding out a lineup that consistently pressured opposing pitching staffs. On the mound, Brody Shawn contributed a key 1–0 record as part of the staff’s depth.

Recognized by D1Baseball and the NCAA with a top-100 RPI ranking, HPU Baseball continues to build a championship-caliber foundation driven by analytics, development, and elite team culture.

About Me

My name is Brody Shawn, and I am proud to be a member of the High Point University baseball program. Along with contributing on the field, I also serve in a leadership role as the team’s Analytics Director.

My work centers around using data to enhance player development, optimize strategy, and support scouting. I specialize in data collection, visualization, and performance analysis — helping bridge the gap between advanced metrics and on-field execution.

Being part of HPU Baseball has shaped my understanding of teamwork, preparation, and innovation. I plan to continue blending my baseball background with analytical skills as I pursue a future in sports analytics.
