About the Team

The High Point University Panthers baseball team competes in NCAA Division I as part of the Big South Conference. Known for a tradition of excellence, our athletes are driven by teamwork, discipline, and leadership.

We focus on player development both on the field and in the classroom. With outstanding coaching, training facilities, and community support, the Panthers continue to rise in the world of collegiate baseball.
