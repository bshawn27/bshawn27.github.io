Pitching Analytics Web App

As a member of the High Point University Baseball Analytics Team, I helped develop a custom application that imports CSV pitching reports from previous outings. The tool automatically organizes and visualizes pitch movement, release trends, velocity, and usage breakdowns, allowing coaches to evaluate performance quickly.

It has become a crucial part of our pitching review process, helping our staff identify strengths, weaknesses, and adjustments needed for future outings.
